OUR TEAM

html website templates

AT ltd team is comprised of experts who have lived in and out of Africa for many years, so developing a deep knowledge of each and every remote corner of this impressive continent most importantly in East African community countries. At African Travellers ltd we passionately believe that bespoke holidays are made special by the people involved, the unique locations of the lodges, and the intimate and personal nature of each trip.

It is with this ethos that AT LTD and our dedicated team plan seamless holidays for discerning clients wanting something out of this world. Our deep love and passion for Africa is portrayed in our manner, and a conversation with us is nothing less than entertaining and exciting, our enthusiasm is totally and utterly infectious.
