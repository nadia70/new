1-day gorilla tour

1-day gorilla tour in Rwanda starting from Kigali to Volcanoes national park, for budget travelers and those on short time plan in Rwanda on conference or volunteering. With Rwanda’s gorilla permits going for $1500, this tour plan is perfect way to save on your gorilla tour budget.

Trip Summary 

1 Day Rwanda Gorilla Tour setting off from Kigali or Musanze driving you to Volcanoes national park for gorilla trekking and back to Kigali.

Package (what is included )

-Gorilla permit/s
– 4*4 Safari Vehicle
– English speaking guide
– Drinking water
– Lunch 

Safari itinerary 

1

The 1 Day short gorilla tour to Volcanoes national park Rwanda sets off by 5:00 am for a 2 hours drive from Kigali to volcanoes national park in Musanze.
Gorilla trekking in Volcanoes is a well managed conservation countrywide initiative for the mountain gorillas, with a set of rules set to protect the endangered species.
Before you head into the forest to search for gorillas, the head guide with brief you about rules and how to conduct when you meet the gorillas and what to expect for the tour. 

2

For your trekking adventure, your guides, porters and rangers go out of their way to make sure you have a memorable experience. The group moves at the pace of the slowest person to accommodation everyone. The guides are very informative and ready to share all the information on gorillas, their behaviors, conservation and the local community.
Chances of seeing gorillas on your trek are high and guaranteed, but just incase you fail to see them, The Uganda Wildlife Authority offers you an opportunity to go back into the forest at half price

3

You have to keep a distance of at least 20 feet away from the gorillas, but as gorillas are wild animals and very curious, they often go up-to the trackers enhancing the experience with the gorillas. Only 8 tourists can track a single gorilla family and visitors are advised to take porters to carry your backpacks as well as help you through tough spots on the hike.

4

You will spend 1 hour with the gorillas, and you return back to the starting point where you will meet your guide. Later have lunch and transfer back to Kigali for drop off at your hotel or airport.

5

End of Tour

2 Days Gorilla Safari Rwanda 

2 Days gorilla tracking Rwanda starts from Kigali with a visit to the Gisozi genocide memorial site where you will learn a lot about the 1994 Rwandan genocide and trekking mountain gorillas in Volcanoes national park. Here you have a great opportunity to select a simple, moderate or tough hike depending on your Physical fitness. Get a chance to watch a family of silverback gorillas unclose on your tour.

Tour Itinerary 

1

Transfer to Volcanoes National Park

This tour starts with an early morning pickup by our company representative from either a hotel / Kigali International Airport for a transfer to Volcanoes National Park, Musanze province. On your way, engage in a Kigali City Tour taking you through Gisozi Genocide Memorial Site before proceeding with your drive to the park. On arrival, proceed to your lodge check in; dine and rest for overnight at The Garden Place(Budget), Le Bambou Gorilla Lodge/ Davinci Gorilla Lodge(Midrange) and Mountain Gorilla View Lodge/ Five Volcanoes Gorilla Lodge(Luxury) 

2

Mountain Gorilla Trek and Return to Kigali

Have breakfast and head for the Kinigi Park offices by 7:00AM; just in time for the morning briefing on the code of conduct when trekking for the mountain gorillas. Later retrieve the starting point; meet up with your guide for drop off at either a hotel / Kigali International Airport.

3

End of Tour
