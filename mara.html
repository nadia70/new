Masai Mara Introduction





PRICING:
From US$1870 in low season to US$2550 in high season per person sharing a classic tent.  (This is subject to change depending on season and availability.)

This is a 5-night introductory safari vacation. Explore the Masai Mara national reserve in Kenya and bear witness to the Great Wildebeest Migration. Enjoy a host of Big 5 activities and stay at Kichwa - situated in the path of the migration.

You'll bear witness to the annual migration of 1.2 million wildebeest plus hundreds of thousands of zebra and Thomson's gazelle, while you can also enjoy a wealth of other safari activities.

Day 1 to 2: Overnight at the hotel of your choice in Nairobi

Once you arrive at Nairobi International Airport, you will be met by our representative and transferred by road to the Hotel. You will overnight at the hotel of your choice in a standard room. There are different hotels each in its own tranquil and private gardens in a short distance from the capital, Nairobi.


Day 2 to 6: Kichwa Tembo Camp, Masai Mara National Reserve

This morning you transfer by road to Wilson Airport for a scheduled light air transfer to the Masai Mara National Reserve. Upon arrival in you are met and road transferred to the camp. You will spend the next 4 nights at Kichwa Tembo Camp on a full board basis joining in shared game viewing activities each day. Kichwa Tembo Camp lies at the base of the Olooloo Escarpment, part of the 'path' of the annual Wildebeest migration. The camp offers luxurious accommodation is small and intimate; and tents have a private viewing deck and wooden doors.


Day 6: Return to Nairobi

Today you transfer by road back to the airstrip and take a scheduled light air transfer to Wilson Airport in Nairobi.  When you land you are met and road transferred to Nairobi International Airport. End of services.
