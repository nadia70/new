1 Day School Tour





There is much fun in visiting the students and it is more fun to visit them while at school.

Day 1 : School Tour.

New Horizon Tours and Services will offer you a unique experience of school Tour visits. There is much fun in visiting students and it is more fun to visit them while at school. Visiting schools like it is in most African settings, students expect to be given pens or soccer balls. Departing in town or village in Rwanda any time of the day for visiting nearby schools, Nursery, primary, and secondary schools.

You will gain an understanding of the Rwandese education system; the walk on foot takes 1 hour for either primary and secondary schools visit. If you want to visit university, let us know. You will basically attend a teaching session then meet the school director and you ask questions about his school or education in general. As extension to this tour, you can choose to volunteer for teaching English in primary or secondary school and this could assist you in boosting your experience.

This package requires a minimum of 3 people with a budget of $100 each. Solo Travelers can be arranged with their visit separately.
